The core technologies behind autonomous AI agents include deep learning algorithms, reinforcement learning, and edge computing. Reinforcement learning enables the AI system to learn from its environment, adapt to new situations, and make decisions in real-time. Edge computing enables the AI system to process data at the edge of the network, reducing latency and improving real-time decision-making. These technologies have been instrumental in the development of autonomous AI agents, enabling the creation of self-sustaining AI systems that can learn, adapt, and interact with their environment without human intervention.
